## RaceSight ##
Real‑Time Race Intelligence for Drivers, Engineers, and Strategy Teams
RaceSight is a dual‑agent race‑intelligence platform engineered to 
operate like a full Formula‑1‑grade strategy, safety, and performance team — all 
inside a single system. It fuses real‑time telemetry, predictive analytics,  
driver‑behavior modeling, and multi‑agent AI reasoning to guide both
 the driver and the pit crew through every second of a race.

RaceSight doesn’t just react.
It anticipates, predicts, adapts, and strategizes.

Its mission is simple:

Help the team win — safely, intelligently, and with precision.

## Core Vision ##
Modern racing is a high‑speed chess match. Winning requires:

millisecond‑level decisions

perfect timing

predictive strategy

safety awareness

and a deep understanding of driver behavior

RaceSight acts as:

a Driver Copilot (micro‑decisions)

a Pit Boss Strategist (macro‑decisions)

a Safety Guardian (risk & compliance)

a Predictive Analyst (tires, fatigue, grip, pace)

a Race Control Assistant (incidents, slow cars, safety car)

a Winning Strategy Engine (real‑time outcome projection)

This is not a dashboard.
This is a race brain.
